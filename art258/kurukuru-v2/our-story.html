OUR STORY

FIRST ESTABLISHED IN 2003

Kuru Kuru Sushi was first founded at Pearl Kai Shopping Center by Tetsuo and Mieko Goto. Lance Yamamoto partnered with them in December 2004 and took over operations to date. It has steadily become a successful restaurant with the help of implemented standards as well as loyal employees and customers.
