ADOPTION

HOW TO ADOPT

To help you and the cats find a good match, potential adopters are required to meet and spend time getting to know the cats before applying to adopt. You can meet the cats by visiting during our regular hours.

FEES

Adoption fees are based on age and set by the Hawaiian Humane Society.

6 months & younger $100
7 months to 5 years $50
6 years + $25
6 years + waived for 55+

HEALTH DETAILS

All of the cats have received the following medical care:

  • Spay/Neuter
  • Microchip
  • Flea/parasite treatment
  • FIV/FeLV Negative
  • Vaccinated

    • Adopters will receive a copy of the medical records.
